About Me

My name is Marcelo Sanchez, I know I already gave an intro of myself. So this can be a continuacion of the story of my life. To make this short and frank, I worked many hours in many companies for lots of hours, days, weeks, months, years(Im sure you get the jist). Suddenly I came to the realization, after speaking with some close successful programmer friends, that I needed to work somewhere that i could be appreciated after putting in the time and knowledge.


All of this is not only for money or the attraction to the craft, but I have 2 little eyes watching my every move. I know the desicions I make, now, govern the future of (not only me) my family. Bottom line is that finally me and my family have everything to look forward to because of this step that I'am unregrettably taking.
